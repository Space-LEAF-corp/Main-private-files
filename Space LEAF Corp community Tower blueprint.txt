🏢 SPACE LEAF CORP TOWER — FLOOR‑BY‑FLOOR BLUEPRINT (v1.0)

A civic sanctuary disguised as a tech tower.

GROUND FLOOR — The Welcome Deck (Public Access)

Purpose: Arrival, grounding, nourishment, orientation.

Key Features:

• Main Lobby Atrium with natural light and plant‑based biowalls
• Digital Café (general meals, coffee, study tables)
• Coin Stop Café Entrance Counter (flatbreads, pizzas, cauliflower crust options)
• Community Info Wall (events, quests, AR missions)
• PokéStop Terminal (digital check‑in, quests, AR anchor)
• Security + Safety Desk (non‑militarized, ceremonial, friendly)
• Elevator Core + Spiral Staircase


Flow:
Enter → Café → PokéStop → Elevators → Upper floors.
