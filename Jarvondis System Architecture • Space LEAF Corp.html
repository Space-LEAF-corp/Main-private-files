02 • Core services

Back-end services that define the Jarvondis “laws of physics”.

backend

Identity service

Handles authentication, authorization, and persona switching (student, mentor, guardian, staff).

  • POST /auth/login, /auth/refresh, /auth/impersonate
  • GET /profiles/:id with lineage, consent, and safety flags

LMS core

Course graph, modules, assessments, and AI-powered learning companions.

  • GET /courses/:id, /courses/:id/modules
  • POST /sessions/:id/events for engagement tracking
  • POST /ai/tutor for context-aware guidance

Messaging & notifications

Unified inbox and multi-channel notifications (in-app, email, SMS, push).

  • POST /messages, GET /threads
  • Event-driven fan-out via message bus (e.g., student.at_risk)
03 • Student & staff apps

Front-ends that sit on top of the core services.

frontend

Student portal

“Digital front door” for Jarvondis: schedule, courses, aid, advising, and campus life.

  • Tech: React / Next.js (web), React Native / Expo (mobile)
  • State: query layer over /lms-core, /advising, /financial-aid

Staff console

Unified view for advisors, faculty, and operations.

  • Role-based dashboards (advising, financial aid, admissions)
  • Embedded analytics from analytics service

Campus orchestrator

Feature flags, experiments, and routing rules for different cohorts and campuses.

  • Config-driven “playbooks” for onboarding, nudges, and interventions

06 • Emotional safety & rituals

Where Jarvondis encodes ceremonies, pause modes, and kid-safe defaults.

culture layer

Safety is treated as code, not vibes. Rituals and constraints live in explicit modules.

  • workflows/safety/ — rate limits, escalation paths, “ceremonial pause” flows.
  • ui-kit/components/SafeZone* — components that enforce copy, tone, and visibility rules.
  • docs/playbooks/ — operational ceremonies for launches, incidents, and recognition.
