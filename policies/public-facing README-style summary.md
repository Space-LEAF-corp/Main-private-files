# Space LEAF Corp — Child Safety Access Policy (Public Summary)

Space LEAF Corp is committed to maintaining a safe, respectful, and welcoming environment for all participants.  
Child safety is a core, non‑negotiable value of this organization.

## What This Policy Does
- Ensures all spaces remain open, warm, and accessible to good‑faith participants.
- Maintains quiet, continuous awareness of behavior patterns that may indicate risk.
- Applies tiered access levels based on demonstrated behavior, not identity or rumor.
- Restricts or removes access for individuals who pose a credible safety concern.

## What This Policy Does NOT Do
- It does not act as law enforcement.
- It does not attempt to detect undocumented crimes.
- It does not accuse anyone without clear, verifiable evidence.
- It does not discriminate based on identity, background, or personal characteristics.

## Our Core Principles
- Child safety comes first.
- Respect and dignity for all participants.
- Decisions are based on observable behavior, not assumptions.
- Transparency, fairness, and documentation guide all actions.
- Everyone has the right to understand decisions that affect their access.

## Access Tiers (Simplified)
- **Full Access:** Consistent respectful behavior.
- **Limited Access:** New or unverified participants.
- **Restricted Access:** Concerning or boundary‑violating behavior.
- **Denied Access:** Verified harm or threats to minors or vulnerable people.

## Commitment
Space LEAF Corp will always prioritize the safety of minors and vulnerable individuals while maintaining a welcoming environment for everyone who participates in good faith.
