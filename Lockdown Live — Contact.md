Lockdown Live — Contact Information

Lockdown Live is the independent media and creative broadcast division within the broader Space LEAF ecosystem.
We produce kid‑safe, humor‑forward, late‑night‑style content with a focus on creativity, digital well‑being, and accessible storytelling.
This document outlines how to reach our team for media inquiries, safety concerns, creative collaboration, and technical questions.

---

🎙️ General Inquiries

For questions about Lockdown Live, our mission, or our public‑facing creative projects:

Email: contact@lockdownlive.org
Website: https://lockdownlive.org

---

🛡️ Safety & Digital Well‑Being

Lockdown Live maintains strict safety standards aligned with Space LEAF Corp’s commitment to ethical technology and protection of minors.

To report safety concerns, misuse, or issues related to Lockdown Live content or community spaces:

Safety Team: safety@lockdownlive.org

Please include:
• A brief description of the issue
• Any relevant screenshots or timestamps
• The platform where the issue occurred

Safety‑related messages are reviewed with priority.

---

🧩 Creative Submissions & Collaboration

Lockdown Live welcomes collaboration with creators, educators, and community partners who share our values of safe, imaginative, and inclusive media.

Creative Partnerships: partners@lockdownlive.org

Please include:
• Your name or organization
• Type of collaboration you’re proposing
• Links to relevant work or materials

Note: We do not accept unsolicited scripts or story pitches without prior agreement.
