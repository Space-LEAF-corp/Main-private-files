6. Symbol Legend (Blueprint Notation)

Tn  = Transmit coefficient (forward)
Rn  = Reflect coefficient (backscatter)
Sn  = Store/recycle coefficient (CERBEA)
G0  = Conductance quantum (2e^2 / h)
α   = Recycling efficiency factor
M   = Number of open lanes
