# Code of Conduct

Thank you for contributing to the GQI&EA project.  
This system is built on clarity, emotional safety, and respectful collaboration.  
All contributors, maintainers, and participants are expected to uphold the values below.

---

## Our Standards

We commit to creating an environment where everyone can participate without fear of:
- Harassment  
- Discrimination  
- Personal attacks  
- Dismissive or hostile behavior  

We encourage:
- Clear, kind communication  
- Constructive feedback  
- Patience with new contributors  
- Respect for different skill levels and backgrounds  
- Transparency in decision-making  
- Collaboration over competition  

---

## Unacceptable Behavior

The following behaviors are not permitted:
- Harassment of any kind  
- Hate speech or discriminatory remarks  
- Threats or intimidation  
- Sharing private information without consent  
- Deliberate sabotage of code, documentation, or systems  
- Disruptive or intentionally harmful actions  

---

## Responsibilities of Maintainers

Project maintainers are responsible for:
- Clarifying standards of acceptable behavior  
- Addressing violations respectfully and consistently  
- Removing or rejecting contributions that violate this Code  
- Protecting the emotional safety of contributors  

Maintainers have the right to remove comments, code, or contributors that violate these guidelines.

---

## Reporting Issues

If you experience or witness behavior that violates this Code:
- Document what happened  
- Contact a project maintainer privately  
- Provide any relevant context  

Reports will be handled discreetly and respectfully.

---

## Commitment to Improvement

This Code of Conduct is a living document.  
We welcome suggestions that strengthen clarity, safety, and collaboration.

Thank you for helping make this project a supportive and resilient space.
