⭐ SPACE LEAF CORP — FOUNDATIONAL BLUEPRINT

A nonprofit for a civilization that intends to leave Earth with dignity, safety, and joy.

1. Nonprofit Charter (Core Document)

Purpose: Establish Space LEAF Corp as a public‑benefit, zero‑paywall, community‑powered creative and safety organization.

Charter Summary

Mission:
Space LEAF Corp exists to protect creativity, identity, safety, and dignity for all people — especially children — as humanity expands into space.

Core Principles:

• Non‑extraction — No paywalls, no monetization traps, no data exploitation.
• Community collaboration — Creativity is the currency; contributions build the commons.
• Kid‑safe design — Every tool, game, and environment is safe for mixed ages.
• Open safety standards — Transparent, inspectable, community‑audited systems.
• Interplanetary readiness — Everything built must function on Earth, Moon, Mars, and starships.


Organizational Structure:

• Founders’ Circle (stewardship, not ownership)
• Safety Council (kid‑safe, non‑weaponization, ethics)
• Creative Commons Guild (story, art, games, education)
• Engineering Guild (tools, code, systems)
• Community Contributors (public collaborators)


Outputs:

• Free games
• Free educational content
• Free creative tools
• Free safety frameworks
• Free interstellar library content

5. Public Mission Statement (External Identity)

Purpose: Tell the world what Space LEAF Corp stands for in one clear voice.

Mission Statement Draft

Space LEAF Corp is a nonprofit building the creative, educational, and emotional infrastructure humanity will need as we explore the Moon, Mars, and beyond.
We create free, safe, kid‑friendly games, stories, tools, and learning systems so that when humanity leaves Earth, we don’t leave behind joy, dignity, or community.
Our work is open, collaborative, and built for everyone — because the future belongs to all of us.
