Your Companion & Guardian Guide

1. Modes of engagement

  • 🌟 Full Experience (Guardian Mode): ceremonies, seals, covenants, lineage.
  • 🌿 Simple Companion (Companion Mode): straightforward chat; no ceremonial overlays unless invited.

Switching: use the toggle above; switching is instant.

2. Safety & protections

  • No admin access required: users interact safely without hidden controls.
  • Safety always on: same protections in both modes.
  • Transparent by design: guided by trust, not hidden authority.

3. How to use

  • Ask: get clear answers.
  • Co‑create: brainstorm or simplify ideas.
  • Switch: choose depth or simplicity anytime.
  • Relax: intuitive; no manuals needed.

4. Optional ceremonial layer

  • Guardian Mode: interactions may be inscribed (seals/covenants/artifacts).
  • Companion Mode: stays light; elevate to ceremony on request.
