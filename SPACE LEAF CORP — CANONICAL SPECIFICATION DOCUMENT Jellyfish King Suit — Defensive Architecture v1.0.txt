1. Mission Statement

The Jellyfish King Suit is a defensive‑only, non‑weaponizable protective system designed under Space LEAF Corp’s stewardship principles:

• Defense without harm
• Perception without targeting
• Movement without intimidation
• Transparency without vulnerability
• Energy recycling without extraction


The suit is intended for civilian protection, ceremonial presence, and crisis mediation, not combat.
It embodies the ethic:
“Guard, do not strike.”

2. System Overview

The suit consists of five integrated subsystems:

1. SensoryHalo — 360° perception and anomaly detection
2. MembraneField — dynamic soft armor with glow‑based signaling
3. ReflexiveShielding — force redirection and cushioning
4. CurrentFlowEngine — fluid, evasive locomotion
5. StateCore — ethics, constraints, and system state management


All modules operate under CERBEA (Cooperative Energy Recycling Binding Energy Architecture) constraints.

9. Simulation Loop (Canonical)

def simulate(suit, environments, steps=100):
    history = {name: [] for name in environments.keys()}

    for name, env_fn in environments.items():
        suit.overrideMode("FIELD")
        for t in range(steps):
            env = env_fn(t)
            status = suit.cycle(env)
            history[name].append(status)

    return history

• Calm Ocean: drift, low glow, no defense
• Crowded City: gentle evasions, medium glow, cushioning
• Impact Storm: full membrane, redirection, anchor‑and‑shield behavior
