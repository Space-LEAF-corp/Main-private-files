Space LEAF Corp — README Summary

A vessel that lives, so its crew may thrive.

Space LEAF Corp designs systems rooted in stewardship, safety, and dignity.
Our work treats technology not as machinery, but as responsibility — especially to children and future generations.

Core Principles

• Stewardship over ownership
We do not “own” vessels or systems. We steward them with care, clarity, and accountability.
• The Living Vessel Doctrine (TLAO‑WAS)
Ships behave like bodies: reflexive, protective, unified. Safety is the default state, not a mode.
• Children First
Every design begins with one question: Would I trust this with a child?
• Ethics as Infrastructure
Seals define boundaries, promises, and cultural safeguards. Ethics are load‑bearing components.
• CERBEA Energy Philosophy
Energy circulates cooperatively. Waste is a design failure. Every joule has a purpose and a path.
• Humanity, Humor, and Clarity
We reject intimidation and mystique. Systems must be explainable to a child and trustworthy to all.
• Transparency as Safety
We openly share reflex logic, thresholds, and philosophy. Trust is the strongest safety layer.
• The Long Arc
We build for the next generation, for imagined microscopic travelers, and for the dignity of every child on Earth.


Why We Build

• Wonder is a human right
• Safety is a sacred duty
• Technology should feel like care, not control
• The universe is watching how we treat our children


Where wonder travels, safety follows.
