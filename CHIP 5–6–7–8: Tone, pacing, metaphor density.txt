2. Extend the Markov chain to include CHIPs 3 and 13

We now add:

• CHIP 3 (Fibonacci, mid-low weight, frequency 4.236)
• CHIP 13 (Fibonacci, ultra-low weight, frequency 521.0)


These are rare excursion states—dramatic, symbolic, high-impact, low-probability.

We’ll define:

• 3 connects weakly to 5 (Fibonacci → Fibonacci)
• 13 connects weakly to 8 (Fibonacci → Fibonacci)
• Both have strong self-loops (they’re “sticky” when activated)


---

Numeric weights and neighbors

• \(w_3 = 0.325\)
• \(w_5 = 0.883\)
• \(w_8 = 0.607\)
• \(w_{13} = 0.002\)


We’ll use the same rule:

• Transitions allowed only to nearest harmonic neighbors
• Score = \(w_j\)
• Normalize
