I. Purpose & Stewardship Mandate

The PCF‑SEA is a sealed, autonomous crystal‑growth capsule designed to use the ocean’s natural hydrostatic pressure as a stabilizing field for long‑duration mineral formation.

It does not mine, scrape, dredge, or disturb the seafloor.
It borrows pressure, grows crystals in silence, and returns whole.

This system is governed by the Seal of Abyssal Stewardship 1.0:
“Pressure is borrowed, never taken. The ocean is a cathedral, not a quarry.”

---

II. System Overview

The PCF‑SEA consists of four primary modules:

1. Abyssal Shell — structural hull & pressure interface
2. Crystal Womb — internal growth chamber
3. Descent & Retrieval Assembly — deployment & recovery
4. Abyssal Ledger — economic & ceremonial registry


Each module is independent, replaceable, and designed for multi‑cycle use.

3. Pressure Multiplication Core

Function:
Raise internal pressure from 0.1 GPa → 1–3 GPa (synthetic gemstone range).

V. Module 3 — Growth Cycle

1. Pressure Phase

Ocean pressure stabilizes the hull.
Internal intensifiers raise chamber pressure to target values.

2. Thermal Phase

• Deep ocean provides natural cooling (2–4°C)
• Internal heaters maintain controlled gradients


3. Crystallization Phase

Growth durations:

• Weeks — small crystals
• Months — medium gemstones
• Years — ceremonial heirloom cores


4. Monitoring

Acoustic telemetry reports:

• Pressure
• Temperature
• Growth rate
• Structural integrity

VI. Module 4 — Retrieval Protocol

1. Recall Signal

Surface buoy sends coded acoustic ping.
Capsule releases ballast and ascends.

2. ROV Retrieval

If needed, ROV attaches a lift line.

3. Surface Recovery

Capsule is hoisted aboard.
Integrity is verified.
Chamber is opened in a controlled lab.

4. Certification

Each crystal receives:

• Depth
• Pressure curve
• Growth duration
• Chemical signature
• Lineage markers


All logged in the Abyssal Ledger.
