Leaf Link Privacy Shield 1.0

Civilian Safety Layer — Space LEAF Corp

---

1. Mission

The Leaf Link Privacy Shield exists to ensure that no human signal can ever be isolated, tracked, or weaponized during satellite uplink communication.
Its purpose is to:

• Protect civilians from being singled out by their digital footprint
• Preserve the integrity of factual, nonviolent information
• Maintain communication continuity during infrastructure failures
• Ensure that identity, location, and content remain separated by design
• Provide a safe, opt‑in channel for people who need to share vital information without exposing themselves


The Privacy Shield transforms the satellite from a potential surveillance vector into a neutral, encrypted relay that cannot identify or target individuals.

---

2. Core Constraints

These constraints are non‑negotiable and define the architecture:

A. No Single‑Signal Isolation

The system must never allow:

• one device → one uplink → one traceable pattern
All traffic is mixed, batched, and anonymized.


B. Metadata Minimization

Only the minimum routing metadata is allowed:

• coarse region
• time window
• temporary channel ID


No precise coordinates.
No persistent identifiers.

C. Separation of Powers

Identity, location, and content must never exist in the same place at the same time.

D. Opt‑In Precision Only

Fine location is only shared when a user explicitly enables Rescue Mode.

E. Auditability

Any elevated access (e.g., rescue) must be:

• logged
• time‑limited
• revocable
• publicly documented


F. No Central Authority

No single node — not even Space LEAF Corp — can unilaterally deanonymize or track a user.

---

3. What Leaf Link Privacy Shield Will Never Do

This is the heart of the spec — the red lines.

❌ Never track individuals

No GPS pinpointing, no device fingerprinting, no behavioral profiling.

❌ Never store personal identity

No names, no phone numbers, no device IDs.

❌ Never allow real‑time targeting

No live location streams.
No tools that could be repurposed for harm.

❌ Never collect data without consent

All participation is voluntary and transparent.

❌ Never act as a surveillance system

The architecture forbids it at the structural level.

❌ Never allow governments, corporations, or third parties to weaponize the network

The system is built to resist misuse — even by powerful actors.

---

5. Summary

Leaf Link Privacy Shield 1.0 is a civilian protection layer that ensures:

• No one can be tracked
• No one can be singled out
• No one can be punished for sharing factual, nonviolent information
• No actor can weaponize the satellite layer
• Communication remains safe even during outages, disasters, or censorship


It is the ethical backbone of the Leaf Link ecosystem.
