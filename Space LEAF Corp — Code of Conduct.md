# 🌱 Space LEAF Corp — Code of Conduct

Space LEAF Corp is dedicated to building technology that protects people, promotes digital well‑being, and supports safe online environments for all ages.  
This Code of Conduct outlines expectations for everyone participating in our projects and community spaces.

Our goal is to maintain a respectful, inclusive, and safety‑first environment where contributors can collaborate without fear of harassment, discrimination, or exploitation.

---

## 🧭 Core Principles

All participants agree to uphold the following principles:

### **1. Safety First**
We prioritize the physical, emotional, and digital safety of all users — especially minors and vulnerable individuals.

### **2. Respect & Kindness**
Treat everyone with dignity.  
No harassment, insults, or demeaning behavior.

### **3. Ethical Technology**
All contributions must support:
- Non‑manipulative design  
- Boundary‑respectful interactions  
- Behavior‑based safety  
- Age‑appropriate content  

### **4. Inclusivity**
We welcome contributors of all backgrounds.  
Discrimination of any kind is not tolerated.

### **5. Transparency**
Communicate clearly, honestly, and constructively.

---

## 🚫 Unacceptable Behavior

The following behaviors are strictly prohibited:

### **Harassment**
- Insults, threats, or personal attacks  
- Derogatory comments  
- Unwanted contact or pressure  

### **Discrimination**
- Racist, sexist, homophobic, transphobic, or ableist language  
- Exclusionary behavior  

### **Exploitation or Manipulation**
- Attempting to bypass safety systems  
- Encouraging unsafe behavior  
- Using the project for harmful purposes  
- Any content that pressures someone to reveal or move their body  

### **Inappropriate Content**
- Explicit, graphic, or sexual content  
- Content unsuitable for minors  
- Content that violates digital body‑safety principles  

### **Disruptive Behavior**
- Spamming  
- Trolling  
- Derailing discussions  
- Intentionally introducing harmful code  

---

## 🛡 Reporting Violations

If you witness or experience a violation of this Code of Conduct:

**Report to:**  
safety@spaceleafcorp.org  
or  
security@spaceleafcorp.org (for technical concerns)

Reports are handled confidentially and with priority.

---

## 🧪 Enforcement

Space LEAF Corp maintainers may take any action they deem appropriate, including:

- Warning the offender  
- Removing comments or content  
- Temporarily or permanently banning contributors  
- Revoking access to repositories or communication channels  

Severe or repeated violations may result in immediate removal.

---

## 🤝 Contributor Responsibilities

By participating in Space LEAF Corp projects, you agree to:

- Follow this Code of Conduct  
- Respect boundaries and safety guidelines  
- Keep all examples age‑appropriate  
- Avoid harmful or manipulative behavior  
- Support a positive, collaborative environment  

---

## 🌐 Maintainer Responsibilities

Maintainers commit to:

- Enforcing this Code consistently and fairly  
- Protecting contributors from harassment  
- Prioritizing user safety  
- Modeling respectful behavior  
- Responding promptly to reports  

---

## 🧭 Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to:

- GitHub repositories  
- Issues and pull requests  
- Discussions and comments  
- Community forums  
- Project meetings  
- Any official Space LEAF Corp communication channel  

---

## 🌟 Our Commitment

Space LEAF Corp exists to build safer digital environments.  
Every contributor plays a role in that mission.

Thank you for helping us create a community rooted in respect, safety, and ethical innovation.
