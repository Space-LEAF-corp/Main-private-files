🜁 1. FULL INTERACTION FLOW

Every tap, gesture, and transition — mapped like a real UI system.

A. Entry Sequence — “The Gate of Beginning”

User Action: Opens the dojo
System Response:

• Screen fades from black to soft seasonal color
• Floating leaf drifts into frame
• Space LEAF Corp sigil pulses once
• Word appears: ENTER


Tap ENTER → Transition:

• Screen inhales (brightness rises)
• Leaf dissolves into particles
• Dojo loads into the current season’s state


---

B. Seasonal Home — “The Mind Wheel”

User Action: Swipes left/right
System Response:

• Wheel rotates with a soft breath‑timed motion
• Seasonal preview animates in miniature
• Ambient sound shifts subtly


Tap a Season → Transition:

• Wheel contracts into a glowing circle
• Circle expands into full dojo environment
• Season‑specific leaf animation begins


---

C. Inside a Season — “The Hall of Practice”

Each season has the same structure, but different emotional tone.

Primary Interactions

• Tap a module → module opens with a soft zoom
• Swipe down → return to seasonal home
• Long‑press → reveals a ceremonial line
• Double‑tap center stage → activates “Still Mode” (ambient only)


Micro‑Interactions

• If user hesitates → a leaf drifts by with a whisper
• If user rushes → the dojo slows animations to guide pacing
• If user becomes still → environment brightens slightly


---

D. Practice Module Flow

Tap a module →

• Title appears in calligraphy
• A single instruction appears (never more than one)
• A soft chime marks the beginning


During practice:

• UI fades away
• Only the environment remains
• Subtle cues guide timing (light, sound, motion)


End of practice:

• A lantern floats upward
• A reflection prompt appears:
“What changed?”
• User can type or skip


---

E. Exiting the Dojo

Swipe from left edge →

• A soft wind animation
• A small leaf appears with the word “Return”
• Tap → fade to black → home screen

🜂 2. ANIMATION SYSTEM FOR SEASONAL SHIFTS

The dojo transforms like a living mind.

A. Transition Philosophy

No hard cuts.
No sudden jumps.
Everything moves like breath.

Inhale → brighten
Exhale → shift environment

---

B. Spring → Summer

• Greens warm into gold
• Budding branch blossoms then dissolves into sunlight
• Mist clears into crisp shadows
• Ambient sound shifts from chimes to heartbeat drums


Symbolic meaning:
Curiosity becomes commitment.

---

C. Summer → Autumn

• Sunbeams soften
• Shadows lengthen
• Leaves begin to fall and dissolve
• Bamboo pillars darken into aged wood


Symbolic meaning:
Heat becomes wisdom.

---

D. Autumn → Winter

• Warm tones drain into silver
• Falling leaves slow until they freeze mid‑air
• Scroll wall fades into frosted windows
• Breath becomes visible


Symbolic meaning:
Reflection becomes clarity.

---

E. Winter → Spring

• Frost melts into dew
• Candle flame becomes morning light
• Frozen leaves thaw and drift upward
• Colors bloom into jade and sky blue


Symbolic meaning:
Stillness becomes openness.

🜃 3. SOUND DESIGN SYSTEM

A ceremonial soundscape that teaches without speaking.

A. Global Sound Principles

• Never intrusive
• Always breathable
• Responds to user pace
• Seasonal, emotional, and symbolic


---

B. Seasonal Sound Profiles

🌱 Spring — Beginner’s Mind

• Soft wind chimes
• Light water ripples
• Occasional lantern bell
• Whisper cue: “Begin with softness.”


☀️ Summer — Focused Mind

• Warm drum heartbeat
• Bamboo clicks
• Sharp but gentle accents
• Whisper cue: “Focus is a form of respect.”


🍂 Autumn — Reflective Mind

• Slow flute
• Falling leaf rustle
• Ink brush strokes
• Whisper cue: “Let go of what no longer serves.”


❄️ Winter — Still Mind

• Deep silence
• Low hum like distant snow
• Single candle crackle
• Whisper cue: “Stillness is the strongest stance.”


---

C. Interaction Sounds

• Tap: soft wood knock
• Swipe: wind pass
• Long‑press: low bell
• Module complete: lantern chime ascending
