---

6. Symbol Key

Tn  = Transmit fraction (forward)
Rn  = Reflect fraction (back to source)
Sn  = Store/recycle fraction (CERBEA)
G0  = Conductance quantum (2e²/h)
α   = Recycling efficiency factor
M   = Number of open lanes
