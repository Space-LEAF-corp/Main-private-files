1. v2.0 full spec sheet

MicroTech Chat: Dictation Stabilizer — Advanced Linguistic Engine v2.0

Module Name

microtech.dictation.stabilizer.advanced

Objective

Refine voice‑to‑text for dialect, accent, and multilingual speakers into clear, readable text without erasing identity, culture, or tone.

Core Capabilities

• Dialect Recognition Layer• Detects regional patterns (AAVE, Southern, Caribbean, UK, AU, etc.)
• Identifies phonetic shifts, dropped consonants, and rhythm patterns

• Phonetic Reconstruction Engine• Maps accent‑driven pronunciations to intended words
• Handles fast speech, slurred articulation, and blended words

• Multilingual Context Engine• Recognizes code‑switching (e.g., English + Spanish)
• Preserves key foreign words while clarifying meaning

• Refinement Modes• Preserve Dialect
• Light Refinement
• Full Refinement
• Dialect → Standard English

• Tone & Identity Lock• Preserves slang, humor, emotional tone
• Locks names, pronouns, and identity markers

• Learning Loop• Adapts to user’s pronunciation over time
• Reduces repeated misinterpretations



Data Flow

1. Raw dictation text from OS
2. Dialect Recognition Layer
3. Phonetic Reconstruction Engine
4. Multilingual Context Engine
5. Tone & Identity Lock
6. Refinement Mode Processor
7. Clean, refined output


Key Functions (Conceptual API)

• analyze_dialect(raw_text, user_profile)
• refine_speech(raw_text, mode, profile)
• lock_identity(name, pronouns, tags)
• learn_pronunciation(audio_features, corrections)
• compare_versions(raw, refined)


Privacy & Ethics

• Local‑first processing
• No cloud training on user identity without explicit consent
• No “sanitizing” of culture or dialect—only clarity refinement
• User can export, reset, or delete their style profile
