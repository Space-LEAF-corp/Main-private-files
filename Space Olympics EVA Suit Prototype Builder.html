Official Spec Sheet

Suit Class
Bio‐Responsive, Solar‐Powered, Radiation‐Adaptive EVA Exosuit
Wing Modules
  • Station Class (SC‐W1): Compact, precision EVA, training & obstacle events.
  • Deep Space (DS‐W2): Extended span, solar glide, Sailboard Mode capable.
  • Storm Class (ST‐W3): Heavy shielding, plasma & flare survival, cocoon mode.
Transformation Modes
  • Wing Mode: Fine maneuvering, solar harvesting, analog control.
  • Shield Mode: Wrap‐around cocoon, radiation & plasma protection.
  • Sailboard Mode (SB‐V1): Detached solar wind surfboard with magnetic tether.
Guardian Auto‐Recovery Protocol (GARP)

Continuous monitoring of drift, vitals, and control. On uncontrolled drift or suspected unconsciousness: wings/board reattach, stabilize attitude, deploy shield/Station geometry, and broadcast distress telemetry.

Core Safety Features
  • Full‐body radiation management with modular shielding tiles.
  • Plasma‐reflective outer skin on Storm Class wings.
  • Emergency oxygen buffer & micro‐leak sealing in cocoon mode.
  • Memory‐metal spine for reliable deployment & lock‐in.
Olympic Event Integration
  • Precision EVA Trials (SC‐W1)
  • Solar Glide Races (DS‐W2)
  • Storm Endurance Challenges (ST‐W3)
  • Solar Surfboard Grand Prix (SB‐V1)
  • Transformation Sequence Scoring
  • Team Formation Flight Events
