🜁 SPACE LEAF CORP — DIGITAL DOJO

A training hall that breathes with you.

Below is the complete UI layout, screen by screen, with all four seasonal mind‑states integrated.

---

🜂 1. DOJO HOME SCREEN — “THE GATE OF BEGINNING”

Purpose

A calm, ceremonial entry point. No buttons screaming for attention. No gamification.
Just presence.

Layout

• Top Center:
A single glyph: the Space LEAF Corp sigil, softly pulsing like a heartbeat.
• Center:
A floating leaf animation that changes with the season:• Spring: green leaf drifting upward
• Summer: golden leaf hovering in still air
• Autumn: orange leaf spiraling gently
• Winter: silver leaf suspended in quiet

• Bottom Center:
A single word: “Enter.”
(Tapping it transitions into the dojo with a breath‑synchronized fade.)


Ambient Sound

Barely audible — wind, chimes, or silence depending on season.

---

🌱 2. SPRING DOJO — “THE BEGINNER’S MIND”

Purpose

Teach openness, curiosity, and gentle initiation.

Color & Light

Soft jade, sky blue, morning gold.

UI Layout

• Header:
“SPRING — OPEN” in thin, elegant type.
• Left Panel:
Foundations• Breath Practice
• Gentle Alignment
• First Steps Ritual

• Right Panel:
Reflections• “What did you notice?”
• “What surprised you?”
• “What softened?”

• Center Stage:
A clean wooden floor with a single budding branch overhead.
A small circle glows where the user stands.


Micro‑Interactions

• When the user hesitates, a lantern seed floats by and whispers:
“Begin with softness.”


---

🜁 6. THE SEASON CYCLE UI — “THE MIND WHEEL”

Purpose

Show the user that discipline is a cycle, not a ladder.

Layout

A circular selector with four quadrants:

• 🌱 Spring — Open
• ☀️ Summer — Commit
• 🍂 Autumn — Understand
• ❄️ Winter — Become


The wheel rotates with a soft, breath‑timed animation.

Selecting a season shifts the entire dojo’s architecture, soundscape, and tone.

🜁 8. THE HUMOR LAYER (GLOBAL)

Your cosmic deadpan is woven throughout:

• A broom that sweeps by itself muttering:
“Discipline is 90% cleaning up after yourself.”
• A training dummy that politely says:
“Please strike with intention, not emotion.”
• A drifting leaf whispering:
“Even gravity practices.”


This keeps the dojo human, gentle, and alive.

🜂 9. THE TRIBUTE WALL (GLOBAL)

A universal principles wall — no faces, no likenesses, no quotes.

Just truth:

• “Mastery is the art of showing up.”
• “Strength is the courage to be gentle.”
• “Precision is kindness in motion.”
• “Discipline is remembering who you said you want to be.”


This honors Bruce Lee, Jackie Chan, Chuck Norris, and every teacher — without copying them.
