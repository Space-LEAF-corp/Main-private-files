# Security Policy — Space Leaf Corp

## 1. Purpose

This document defines how to report vulnerabilities or misuse related to the Giga Atom project.

## 2. Reporting a Vulnerability

If you discover a vulnerability, misuse, or potential weaponization vector:

- Email: [security@spaceleafcorp.org](mailto:security@spaceleafcorp.org)
- Subject line: "SECURITY REPORT — GIGA ATOM"
- Include:
  - Description of the issue
  - Steps to reproduce
  - Potential impact
  - Suggested mitigation (if known)

## 3. Response Timeline

- Acknowledgment within 72 hours.
- Initial assessment within 7 days.
- Full resolution timeline depends on severity.

## 4. Prohibited Disclosure

Do NOT publicly disclose vulnerabilities.
Do NOT share findings with third parties.
All reports are confidential under the Space Leaf Corp Proprietary License.

## 5. Enforcement

Any attempt to exploit, weaponize, or misuse vulnerabilities will result in:

- Immediate revocation of access
- Civil penalties
- Legal action under U.S. and international law

## 6. Mission Alignment

All security work must support the mission of **planetary protection, safety, and nonprofit benefit**.
