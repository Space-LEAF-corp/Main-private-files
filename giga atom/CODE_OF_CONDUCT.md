## 1. Mission Alignment
All contributors must uphold the mission of Space Leaf Corp:
- Protecting planetary wellbeing
- Advancing safe, ethical research
- Preventing harm, misuse, or weaponization

## 2. Expected Behavior
Contributors must:
- Act with integrity, humility, and stewardship
- Communicate respectfully and professionally
- Prioritize safety and ethical responsibility
- Report concerns immediately

## 3. Unacceptable Behavior
- Harassment, discrimination, or hostile conduct
- Attempts to weaponize or misuse the project
- Unauthorized redistribution of proprietary materials
- Sabotage, backdoors, or malicious code
- Any action that violates the Space Leaf Corp Proprietary License

## 4. Enforcement
Violations may result in:
- Removal from the project
- Revocation of access
- Legal action where applicable

## 5. Reporting
Report violations to:
conduct@spaceleafcorp.org

All reports are confidential.
